## Tried AI and Gave Up?

If you've already tried AI tools and walked away thinking, "This isn't for me," you're not alone. Most people give up too early---not because they're doing anything wrong, but because no one shows them how to use these tools in a real, useful way. It's not magic. It's technique.

The secret to making AI work? Start small. Ask it to help with things you already do---writing, organizing, thinking. Build trust one task at a time.
